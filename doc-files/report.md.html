Code: 1. Write a planet generator that generates a low-poly planet using three heightfields. These heightfields correspond to the water, low-level terrain, and mountains. 2. Use a noise function to generate these heightfields. 3. Generate a random background, "Sky", for better aesthetic. 4. Add starship models and other accessories to the scene to make it look realistic. 5. Add a GUI that allows for adjustments of the planet, such as biomes and/or themes, size, flatness, etc. 2. Correctness Results: 1. Show a low-poly planet with no terrain. 2. Show a planet with only one terrain. 3. Show one example of a planet with various terrains. 3. Quality Results: 1. A scene of a planet with some models and an interesting background. 2. A scene of a planet with a moon rotating around it. 3. A unique scene demonstrating the beauty of our code.
